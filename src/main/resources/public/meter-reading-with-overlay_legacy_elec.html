How to read all Standard meters, including dial and digital meters

How to read your Standard or Wattless meter

Standard (24hr) and Wattless meters both have one row of numbers. This row is labelled as kWh on Standard meters and as kVArh on Wattless meters but you can read both of them and submit your reading in the same way.

To read your meter:

  1. Read the numbers from left to right
  2. Ignore any numbers after the decimal point or in red

How to read your Digital/Electronic meter

Digital Meters have an electronic display which changes every 9 seconds. You may also have a "Scroll" button which allows you to manually change the screens.

To read your meter:

  1. Scroll or wait until your digital meter shows the following screen:

    e.g., The reading for this meter would be 5812
  2. Write down the numbers from left to right
  3. Ignore any numbers after the decimal point

Don't see your meter or still need help?

If you don't see your meter here try our Meter Reading Guide, or if you have any other meter reading issues just contact Customer Services.
