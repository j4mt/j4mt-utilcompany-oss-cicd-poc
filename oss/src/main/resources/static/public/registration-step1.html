Your account has already been registered. If you know your details you can login below, otherwise you could reset your password to log in, or contact Customer Service for assistance.

Please enter your SSE Airtricity account number. This is a 6 or 7 digit number you can find on your initial welcome letter, in your eBill notification emails or at the top of your paper bill.

Why register for My SSE Artricity?

  • Our online service gives you full control of your SSE Airtricity account and billing.
  • Submit meter readings to keep your bills accurate and costs down.
  • You can access your account whenever or wherever you like to view your bills and make payments online.
  • Track your energy consumption and save money with online billing.
