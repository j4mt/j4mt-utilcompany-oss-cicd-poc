If your circumstances have changed and you're finding it hard to make payments, the best thing to do is to let us know as soon as possible. By putting it off you'll reduce your payment options, and be at risk of bigger charges and higher rates.

You can always contact us in confidence, and we'll do everything we can to help.

Payments received at least 6 working days before your next bill date will be credited to that bill. If the payments you made were:

  • More than your bill amount: any remaining credit will stay in your account and roll over to your next bill.
  • Less than your bill amount: you'll have to pay the remaining balance just like a normal bill.

Top-up payments will take up to 3 working days to show up in your online account, and you can make them using any of our available payment options.

This is the date that your direct debit will be taken out of your account. Any extra payments you make up to 5 days before this date will be taken off the balance of your direct debit, payments made after this will be credited to your next bill.

If you've recently changed the date of your direct debit, requested a new bill or had an unpaid direct debit, the date shown here may not be accurate.

If you're having difficulty paying your bill, it's important to get in touch with us as soon as you can so we can work out a payment plan. [Learn more about your payment options.]

Any charges associated with the failure to make a payment due to insufficient funds will appear on your next bill.

You make regular payments each month based on your predicted energy costs for the coming year.

This is how much energy usage you have been charged for during this budget plan period.

This difference will gradually be added onto your budget payments for the period, until the amount is paid off.

This difference will always be in credit or debit as you're making regular payments each month instead of paying for the actual energy you used.

You'll pay a little more than you use during Summer in order to pay a lot less than you use during Winter.
